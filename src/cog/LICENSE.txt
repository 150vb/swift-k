Project Registration
----------------------------------------------------------------------

We kindly ask you to notify us about projects that you develop with
the help of the Java CoG Kit. This will allow us to keep track of the
use of the Java CoG Kit, as this directly affects our ability to
motivate additional coding activities. Please, either register through
the 

  cog-setup 

component, or simply go to 

  http://www.cogkit.org/register/

In case you have additional questions, please do not hazitate to
contact gregor@mcs.anl.gov.
